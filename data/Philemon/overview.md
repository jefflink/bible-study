## 🗺️ Background & Context

| Element | Details |
|---|---|
| **Who is Philemon?** | A wealthy Christian man, likely from Colossae, whose house served as a church meeting place |
| **Who is Onesimus?** | Philemon's runaway slave who had wronged his master and fled to Rome |
| **Who is Paul?** | The Apostle, writing from prison — he led Onesimus to faith in Christ while imprisoned |
| **The Situation** | Onesimus became a Christian under Paul's ministry. Paul sends him back to Philemon with this letter, appealing for mercy and reconciliation |
