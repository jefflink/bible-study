Biblical Timeline

Primeval History
c. 4000 BC
Creation
God creates the heavens and the earth in six days. (Gen 1)
c. 3000 BC
The Flood
Noah builds the ark; God floods the earth and establishes His covenant. (Gen 6–9)
Patriarchal Period
c. 2091 BC
Abraham's Call
God calls Abram out of Ur; the Abrahamic covenant is established. (Gen 12)
c. 1850 BC
Joseph in Egypt
Joseph is sold into slavery and rises to become second-in-command. (Gen 37–50)
The Exodus & Law
c. 1446 BC
The Exodus
Moses leads Israel out of Egypt; the Law is given at Sinai. (Exod 1–20)
Kingdom Period
c. 1010 BC
David Becomes King
David unites Israel and establishes Jerusalem as the capital. (2 Sam 5)
c. 966 BC
Solomon's Temple
The first temple is built in Jerusalem. (1 Kgs 6)
New Testament
c. 4 BC
Birth of Jesus
The Word becomes flesh and dwells among us. (Luke 2, John 1:14)
c. AD 30
Crucifixion & Resurrection
Christ dies for the sins of the world and rises on the third day. (John 19–20)
c. AD 95
Revelation Written
John receives the final vision of Christ's return and the new creation. (Rev 1)
